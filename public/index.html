Whispered Love Letter

Love can manifest in our lives without us having a say in this.

Love can linger and overstay its welcome.

Love can fade leaving us to question what the fuzz was all about.

Was it love?

Think of love (or any of its manifestations) in your life.

This is a place where you can share and let go of those feelings, emotions, and thoughts that need to be acknowledged but you haven't found a way to set free.

This place has no memory. Everything you share here will dissolve and disappear.
